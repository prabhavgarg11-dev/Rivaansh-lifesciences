Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery is 2-3 business days. Express same-day delivery is available in select areas.

Do you deliver prescription medicines?

Yes. Upload a valid prescription and our pharmacist team will verify before dispatch.

Can I return a product?

Non-prescription items are eligible for return within 7 days if unopened and in original condition. Prescription items are not returnable due to safety guidelines.

How do I track my order?

Use the order tracking section after checkout or click Track Order in the footer for updates.
