How do I upload a prescription?

Open any Rx-required product, click “Upload Prescription”, choose your file, and submit. Our pharmacy team validates the document within 1 business day.

What is auto-refill?

When you subscribe for a product, we save your schedule and automatically prepare the next order based on therapy frequency. You can manage or cancel anytime from “Subscriptions”.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled anytime before they are dispatched. Once an order is out for delivery or shipped, cancellation is not possible. For prescription medicines, cancellations are allowed until the prescription verification process is complete.

2. Returns & Refunds

Due to the sensitive nature of pharmaceutical products, returns are only accepted if:

  • The product delivered is past its expiry date.
  • The product is damaged during transit.
  • An incorrect item was delivered.

In such cases, please notify us within 24 hours of delivery with photographic evidence.

3. Refund Timeline

Once a return is approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Refund & Returns Policy

1. Eligibility

Refunds are available within 7 days of delivery for unopened, undamaged medicines. Controlled substances and custom orders are non-refundable.

2. Return Process

Contact us at rivaanshlifesciences@gmail.com with your order ID and reason. We'll provide a prepaid return label. Once received and verified, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

3. Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. PayPal and credit card refunds may take 1-3 business days to appear.

Opened medicines, expired products, or items damaged due to mishandling cannot be returned.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive damaged or incorrect medicines, report within 24 hours. We'll replace or refund the item immediately at no cost.

Refunds may be reduced for return shipping costs or restocking fees (typically 10%) unless the return is due to our error.
